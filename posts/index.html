LIME

LIME is a local interpretability technique that uses a simpler surrogate model like linear regression or decision tree, to explain the original complex target model**. It is a model agnostic method, meaning it does not depend on the underlying nature of the target model, and that makes this technique very flexible to accommodate any kind of machine/deep learning architectures.
